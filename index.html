About Us

We are Dimitris Anagnostopoulos and Alexandros Antonelakis, two professional private chefs based in Chania, Crete.

We have both participated in MasterChef Greece (Seasons 7 & 9), an experience that sharpened our culinary identity, discipline and creativity.

We specialize in luxury, tailor-made dining experiences in villas and private residences.

From menu design and sourcing to execution and full kitchen clean-up, we manage every detail.
